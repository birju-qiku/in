13MP Intelligent
Dual Rear Cameras
Reinvention for A Better
Experience

Image enhancement by increasing the size of the camera light sensor requires a larger camera module. This leads to a slightly protruding camera that reduces the attractiveness of the phone's exterior. If the size of the light sensor is fixed and only the pixels are upgraded to improve image quality, the amount of light per unit area for each pixel will decrease. As a result, the camera cannot capture the best quality images and include every little detail. After nearly a year of R&D, QiKU has taken an innovative step forward to introduce the concept of Intelligent Dual Rear Cameras smart design in Sony IMX278 color sensor and IMX214 Mono Black&White sensor.

13MP Intelligent Dual Rear Cameras
Bringing You New Horizons

The QiKU phone uses the Sony IMX278 RGBW CMOS Image Sensor and IMX214 MONO Black&White night vision sensor. The first feature adds a white pixel to every pixel, improving the amount of light by 40% and removing 78% of noise from any image or video captured in low-light environments. It is specifically designed to capture color images. The second feature is responsible for outlines and details, and supports four times more light than a common color sensor. Coupled with deep refinement through double-camera real-time processing, these features present colorful and highly detailed photos.
