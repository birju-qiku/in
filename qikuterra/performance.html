Exclusive Space Cooling Process
Better to Solve Android’s Heating Problem

The space cooling mechanism applied in Terra and Terra prime is to cool the phone down when it runs intensely. The cooling process works the same way as satellites’ cooling process. Inside the phone structure, there is a tiny pipe inserted with flowing liquid, flowing to high temperature end going diffused, flowing to low temperature end going coagulated. With this circulation we ensure both ends temperature are well balanced and the handset doesn’t get that heat. Free play and play hard.

Eye Protection Mode

The light from computers and mobile phones comes primarily from the short-wave, high-energy, blue end of the visible light spectrum. This near-UV light can pass through the eye's crystalline lens and reach the retina, irreversibly damaging the epithelial cells on the retina. QiKU Terra can filter most blue light flux emitted by the phone. Your eyes won't feel dry even after staring at the screen for a long time.

Turbo Fast Charge
10 Minutes Charging = 4 Hours Call

Turbo engines have better fuel efficiency by increased air pressure and
density due to raising the cylinder charge level. Correspondingly, the fuel
used is increased and the motor speed is adjusted, resulting in a higher power
output. Similar to a turbo engine, the QiKU Turbo Fast Charge technology
increases current output (2.2 A) by increasing voltage (9 V) to supply
more power to the phone, substantially improving charge speed.

Dual band Wi-Fi
Fast Internet Surfing

The QiKU phone adopts the 802.11a/b/g/n/ac standard and supports 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz Wi-Fi. They feature more stable and faster transmission than single band Wi-Fi. The entry-level speed of 5 GHz Wi-Fi is 433 Mbps, which is more than three times the speed of common Wi-Fi, ensuring smooth grid communication.
